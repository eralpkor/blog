About Me

This is what I do.

I'm a front-end developer who is passionate about Javascript, building great user experiences, fighting for simplicity over complexity, constantly learning and sharing with other developers around me.

I've had 16+ years experience as a Hardware Test Engineer, starting out as a IT Help Desk Technician for SonicWall Firewalls. After that I continued my journey at IBM as a Hardware Test Engineer. About two years ago I started to teach myself web technologies; CSS, HTML and JavaScript.

I enjoy learning new frameworks, libraries and programming philosophies. My interests primarily lie in design and implementation of native web apps, whether desktop or mobile, single-page applications or otherwise. Although, I primarily focus on Javascript (ES5/6, node & browser), HTML5 and CSS3.

I also enjoy photography, I like taking pictures of landscapes and my cats.
